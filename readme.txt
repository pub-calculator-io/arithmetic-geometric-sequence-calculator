Number sequence calculator to find the nth term of arithmetic, geometric, and Fibonacci sequences. The calculator also finds the sum of the terms of a sequence.

[https://www.calculator.io/arithmetic-geometric-sequence-calculator/](https://www.calculator.io/arithmetic-geometric-sequence-calculator/)

== Usage ==

Add the Arithmetic and Geometric Sequence Calculator shortcode to your page, post or sidebar:

`[ci_arithmetic_geometric_sequence_calculator]`

Add the following code to your template where you would like the Arithmetic and Geometric Sequence Calculator to appear:

`<?php display_ci_arithmetic_geometric_sequence_calculator(); ?>`
